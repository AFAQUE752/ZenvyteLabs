Terms of Service

Read before ordering.

These terms protect both you and us. Know what you're agreeing to before opening a ticket.

💳

Full Upfront Payment

Payment in full is required before any work begins. No partial payments or pay-after-delivery arrangements.

🚫

No Refunds After Work Starts

Once work has begun, no refunds will be issued. Make sure you're certain about the scope before paying.

⏱

Delivery Time Varies

Delivery depends on project complexity and current workload. An estimated timeline is always provided before payment.

📝

Clear Requirements Required

You must provide clear, detailed requirements upfront. Vague briefs may delay the project or require a revised quote.

✏️

Major Revisions Cost Extra

Minor adjustments are included. Significant changes to scope or direction after work has started will be quoted separately.

💤

7+ Days Inactivity = Project Closed

If a client goes unresponsive for 7 or more days without notice, the project will be closed with no refund.

🖥

Not Responsible for Hosting

We provide server setups and development only. We are not responsible for your host's performance, downtime, or hardware.

⛔

Right to Refuse Service

We reserve the right to decline or cancel any order at our discretion, particularly for abusive or unreasonable requests.

Note: By opening a ticket and making payment, you acknowledge and agree to all of the above. These terms apply to every order without exception.
